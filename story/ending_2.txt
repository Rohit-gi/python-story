MISSION SUCCESSFUL

Having navigated through twists and choices, the friends stand at the crossroads of a greater revelation. The mystical door, reveal a deeper connection, signaling an impending 
darkness that transcends the boundaries of the Three Realms. Strengthened by their victories, the unexpected twists, and the choices that defined their journey, 
Jake, Alex, Maya, and Ethan prepare to face the shadows in the unfolding chapters of their mystical odyssey.
