THE FROSTY ADVENTURE

Choosing the door adorned with frosty symbols, Jake leads the group into a world of towering peaks and freezing winds. In a surprising twist, they discover a hidden passage 
leading to a cavernous ice chamber. Frostbite, the icy super villain, awaits. Donning thermal suits, the friends confront Frostbite and uncover the ancient Gauntlet of Ember.
